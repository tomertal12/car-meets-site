About Car Meets

Car Meets is a mobile application designed for automotive enthusiasts worldwide. Our platform connects car lovers, helping them discover local meetups, car shows, races, and automotive events in their area.

Whether you're into classic cars, supercars, JDM, or just love automobiles, Car Meets helps you find your community and never miss an event.

Email Communication Policy

Car Meets uses email exclusively for essential transactional and account-related communications. We are committed to responsible email practices and user privacy.

Types of Emails We Send:

  • Account Verification: One-time verification codes when users sign up or verify their email address
  • Password Reset: Secure password reset links requested by users
  • Security Notifications: Important alerts about account security (e.g., unusual login activity)
  • Account Updates: Critical notifications about account status or terms changes

Our Commitments:

  • We never send promotional or marketing emails
  • We never share or sell user email addresses to third parties
  • Users can only receive emails after explicitly signing up in our app
  • All emails include clear identification of our service
  • We maintain low email volume - only essential communications

Privacy & Data Protection

We take user privacy seriously. Personal information collected through our app is used solely to provide our services and improve user experience.

  • User data is encrypted and stored securely
  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations
  • Users can request account deletion at any time
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties
